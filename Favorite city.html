Cities



Pune


Pune exemplifies an indigenous Marathi culture and ethos, in which education, arts and crafts, and theatres are given due prominence.


Good places to visit

  • Aga Khan Palace(Museum)
  • Shaniwar Wada(Historical landmark)
  • Sinhagad Fort(Fortress)

Mumbai


Mumbai is located in the state of Maharashtra. It is famously known as the capital of Maharashtra and the financial capital of India.

Earlier it was known as Bombay. Its name is derived from the name of the goddess Mumba (a form of Parvati) who is the goddess of

fishermen.


Good places to visit

  • Gateway Of India Mumbai(Arch-monument)
  • Elephanta Caves(City of Caves)
  • Marine Drive(Promenade)

Jabalpur


The city of Jabalpur - situated at the heart of Madhya Pradesh - is a very popular place amongst tourists. This city is surrounded by

low hills and lies north of the River Narmada.


Good places to visit

  • Bhedaghat(Nagar Panchayat)
  • Bargi Dam(Dam)
  • Rani Durgawati Fort(Historical Fort)
